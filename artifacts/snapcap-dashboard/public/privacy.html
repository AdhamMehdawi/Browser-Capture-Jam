VeloCap — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-23

VeloCap is a developer tool distributed as a Chrome extension and an accompanying web dashboard. This policy explains what data the extension collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

What the extension captures

The extension only captures data when you explicitly click "Record". While recording, on the tab you select, it captures:

  • A screen recording of that tab
  • Console log output from that tab
  • Outbound network requests and their responses from that tab
  • User interactions on that tab (clicks, input events, navigations) — input values are masked
  • Page URL and title, referrer, and basic browser/device metadata (user agent, viewport, language, timezone)

The extension does not run background data collection. It does not read browsing history, cookies, passwords, or data from tabs other than the one you actively record.

Authentication

To upload recordings to your VeloCap account, you sign in via Clerk. Clerk stores your email, name, and (if you choose) your Google profile. The extension only receives a session token from Clerk; it does not see your password.

Where data is stored

Recordings are uploaded to servers hosted on Microsoft Azure in the UAE North region. Specifically:

  • Recording metadata, console/network logs, and user-action trails are stored in a private PostgreSQL database.
  • Video and screenshot binaries are stored in Azure Blob Storage.
  • Access is gated by your Clerk-authenticated session.

Who can access your recordings

By default, only the user who created a recording can view it. A recording becomes accessible to others only if you explicitly generate and share a share link. Share links can be revoked at any time from the dashboard.

Third parties

VeloCap uses these third-party services:

  • Clerk — authentication (Clerk privacy policy)
  • Microsoft Azure — hosting (Microsoft privacy statement)

VeloCap does not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or any other third parties.

Retention and deletion

Recordings remain until you delete them from the dashboard. You can delete any of your recordings at any time. When you delete a recording, its database row and associated video/screenshot blobs are removed.

Your rights

You can:

  • View and delete any of your recordings from the dashboard
  • Revoke any share links you have generated
  • Request full account deletion by contacting the email below

Contact

For privacy questions or account-deletion requests: info@menatal.com

This policy applies to the VeloCap Chrome extension and the VeloCap dashboard. It does not apply to the websites you record — those are governed by their own policies.

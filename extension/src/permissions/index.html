OpenJam · Microphone

We record your voice-over via your browser's microphone. This permission is granted to the extension once; recording happens locally and is uploaded only as part of a Jam you create.

macOS: you also need to enable Chrome in System Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone. After enabling there, quit and reopen Chrome.

Previously blocked? Open chrome://settings/content/microphone, find this extension in the Not allowed list, and remove it — then click the button above again.

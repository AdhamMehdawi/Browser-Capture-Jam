OpenJam · Screen Recording permission

macOS blocks all screen & tab capture until you explicitly enable it for Chrome — even for recording a single tab. Fix it in three steps:

1. Open System Settings → Screen Recording
  1. In the list that opens, find Google Chrome and turn the toggle on. (If it's already on, toggle it off and back on to refresh macOS's cache.)
  2. macOS will ask "Quit & Reopen". Click it. If it doesn't ask, fully quit Chrome with ⌘Q — not just closing the window — and reopen it.
  3. Go to chrome://extensions, click reload on the Velo QA card, then try recording again.
Not seeing the button above open System Settings?
Chrome may block the x-apple.systempreferences: link. Open System Settings manually, search for Screen Recording, and enable Chrome there.
